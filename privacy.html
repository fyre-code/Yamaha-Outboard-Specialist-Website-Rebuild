Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Introduction

Yamaha Outboard Specialist (“Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, request service, or communicate with us. By using our website or contacting us, you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, including your name, phone number, email address, service address, engine model or serial number, and details about your service needs. We may also collect limited technical data such as IP address, browser type, and website usage information for analytics and website improvement purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, schedule diagnostics and repairs, provide estimates and invoices, send appointment confirmations and updates, communicate about approvals or service status, improve our services, and maintain business records. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

SMS Communications Consent (A2P/TCPA)

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive text messages from Yamaha Outboard Specialist for service-related communications. These may include appointment confirmations and reminders, service updates, diagnostic approvals, billing notifications, and responses to inquiries. If you separately opt in, you may also receive occasional promotional messages such as service reminders or announcements. Message frequency varies and message and data rates may apply. You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, you may receive one final confirmation message. For assistance, reply HELP or contact us at 321-604-7257. Consent to receive text messages is not required to make a purchase, but certain service-related communications may be necessary to fulfill a service request you initiate. Mobile information will not be shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

How We Share Information

We may share limited information with trusted service providers such as SMS and phone providers, email platforms, website hosting providers, payment processors, or scheduling systems solely to operate our business. These providers are required to protect your information and use it only for business purposes on our behalf. We do not sell personal information.

Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no online transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies or analytics tools to improve user experience and understand website traffic. This information does not personally identify you unless you voluntarily submit it through a form.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites such as social media platforms or map services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party sites.

Your Rights and Choices

You may request access to your personal information, request corrections, or request deletion of your information subject to applicable legal obligations. To make such a request, contact us at 321-604-7257.
