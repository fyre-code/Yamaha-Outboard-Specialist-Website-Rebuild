Who We Are

Yamaha Outboard Specialist is a dedicated boat repair and outboard service business based in Melbourne, FL, specializing in maintenance, repair, and support for Yamaha, Mercury, Honda, Suzuki, and Tohatsu outboard motors and related marine equipment.

Focused on quality service and expertise for outboards, we serve recreational boat owners and marine enthusiasts throughout Brevard County and the surrounding coastal communities.

Our business supports customers with professional diagnostics, repairs, and part installations, helping keep your boat running smoothly. We also do some jet ski maintenance work as well.

Our approach is built on clear communication, honest diagnostics, and repairs that are done right—not quick patches that fail later.
